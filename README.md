# PBS Projects

The official website for PBS Projects, a glass and aluminum installation
business based in Harare, Zimbabwe, with 3+ years of experience in the
trade.

## About PBS Projects

PBS Projects designs, builds, and fits aluminum and glass work for homes,
shops, and offices: windows, doors, shower cubicles, shop fronts,
suspended ceilings, and custom cabinets. Based in Waterfalls, Harare, and
serving customers across the city.

## Why this site exists

Before this, PBS Projects was found the way most small trades are, word
of mouth, referrals, and whoever happened to have the right WhatsApp
number. That works, but it puts a ceiling on how many people ever hear
about the business, and it gives a new customer nothing to look at
before deciding to reach out. This site is a real, direct presence
online: real photos of real completed jobs, a clear way to request a
quote without needing an introduction first, and a place for past
customers to leave a genuine review for the next person deciding
whether to call.

## What the site offers

- A homepage introducing the business and its work
- A real gallery of completed jobs, organized by category, including
  photos and video
- A products and services overview
- A quote request form that goes directly into a real lead pipeline, so
  nothing gets missed
- Customer testimonials, submitted directly and reviewed before they go
  public
- Contact details and a map to the workshop
- English and Shona language support, for a site that actually reads
  naturally for its own local customers
